    Our Journey

    Milestones That Defined Us

    Each year brought new ambitions, harder challenges, and prouder deliveries.

    2006
    The Beginning
    Founded with a 12-unit apartment project in Adyar, Chennai. Sold out in 48 hours.
    2010
    First Villa Community
    Launched Azure Shores — 40 premium villas along ECR. Set the benchmark for coastal luxury.
    2015
    National Expansion
    Entered Bengaluru, Hyderabad and Kochi markets. 500 families housed by year-end.
    2019
    IGBC Green Certified
    All new projects mandated to meet IGBC Gold Standard — a first among Chennai developers.
    2024
    2,400 Families & Counting
    Crossed the landmark with The Pinnacle penthouse collection — our finest work yet.
